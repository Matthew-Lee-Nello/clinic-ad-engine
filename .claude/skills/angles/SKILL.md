# angles

Produce 25 to 30 genuinely distinct angles, each a different reason to convert. This is the heart of the Jeremy Haynes method: messaging diversification, not variation. Output to `clients/<slug>/02-angles.md`.

Read `clients/<slug>/01-research.md`, `knowledge/haynes-method.md`, and `knowledge/singapore-ad-compliance.md` first.

## What "distinct" means

Distinct angles are different REASONS, not the same reason reworded. Vary along:

- **Buyer-spectrum stage:** unaware (educate the problem exists), problem-aware (name the frustration), solution-aware (explain the mechanism), provider-aware (the doctor's specific process).
- **Mechanism:** the how and why of the treatment, the science, the technique.
- **Objection dissolved:** cost, downtime, pain, safety, "will it work for me", "is it worth it".
- **Audience segment:** different people who want the outcome for different reasons.
- **Emotional driver:** confidence, relief, normality, function, prevention.
- **Format of the argument:** myth vs fact, a question answered, a process walkthrough, a "who this is for", a day-in-the-life of the condition.

Because Singapore compliance bans before/afters, testimonials, superlatives, and results claims, the angles must win on education, mechanism, specificity, and the doctor's real authority. That is the whole point.

## The compliance gate (run on every angle before keeping it)

Check each angle against `knowledge/singapore-ad-compliance.md`:
- No before/after or after-only treatment imagery implied.
- No testimonial, review, or endorsement.
- No superlative or laudatory word.
- No results claim or unjustified expectation.
- No comparison or deprecation of other clinics.
- Not written to solicit or pressure.
- Every factual claim substantiable by the clinic.

If an angle cannot pass, revise it or drop it. Never keep a non-compliant angle to fill a slot.

## Output (`02-angles.md`)

A numbered list of 25 to 30 angles. For each:
- **Angle name** (short).
- **Buyer-spectrum stage** it targets.
- **The core idea** in one or two sentences (the reason to convert).
- **Media fit:** static, video, or both.
- **Compliance note:** confirmed clear, plus any wording to avoid.

Then a **Dropped** section: angles you considered and cut, with the compliance reason. This transparency is a feature, the doctor needs to see what was cut and why.

Aim for 30 so that after the compliance gate you still have a strong 25 plus.
