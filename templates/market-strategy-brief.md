# Market & Strategy Brief - <clinic> / <treatment> / <country>

## 1. Client core
- **Doctor / clinic:** name, credentials, HCSA licence status.
- **Point of difference (real, substantiable):** what this doctor genuinely does better or differently.
- **Strengths to lead with:** technique, technology, training, process, factually stated.
- **What the client wants to market:** this treatment, and why now.
- **Assets on hand:** doctor media, footage, brand (from the Drive folder).
- **Funnel + country + language.**

## 2. Treatment
- What it is, in plain terms, and the patient journey (trigger → research → decision).
- The real questions and objections the market has (cost, downtime, pain, safety, "will it work for me").
- Country-specific nuances for this treatment.

## 3. Market sizing (TAM / SAM / SOM)
Per `knowledge/tam-methodology.md`. Show the factor tables with sources/assumptions.
- **TAM:** annual market value + working.
- **SAM:** reachable slice.
- **SOM:** realistically winnable in 12 months, with the bottom-up funnel cross-check.
- **Verdict:** is demand, budget, or delivery the constraint; is this treatment worth launching now.

## 4. Competitor landscape + swiped concepts
- **Who is running** (Meta Ad Library, niche + country): advertiser, longevity, media type, angle, compliant-here yes/no.
- **Swiped concepts** (cross-market, per `knowledge/swipe-method.md`): source, the transferable concept, why it works, our compliant adaptation, which angle territory it feeds.
- Honest note if the niche library is thin (expected in restricted markets) and where concepts were swiped from instead.

## 5. Positioning + the wedge
- **Market gaps:** where competitors are generic, weak, or non-compliant.
- **The doctor's leverage:** where the POD meets a gap the market cares about.
- **Buyer spectrum:** awareness stage and sophistication stage of this market (Schwartz), and the messaging implication (claim vs mechanism-led).
- **The positioning line:** how we frame this clinic + treatment, compliantly.

## 6. The game plan (highest-probability play)
- **Angle territory:** the themes the 25-30 angles will come from (feeds the `angles` stage).
- **Recommended structure:** budget read, ad-set setup, what to test first.
- **The reasoning:** why this gives the most probable chance of working for THIS treatment.
- **Open risks + what would change the plan.**
